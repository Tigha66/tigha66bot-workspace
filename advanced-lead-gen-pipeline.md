## Lead Scoring Model

| Score | Priority | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| 8-10 | 🔴 HIGH | Immediate outreach, personalized video |
| 6-7 | 🟡 MEDIUM | Standard email sequence |
| 4-5 | 🟢 LOW | Nurture campaign |
| 0-3 | ⚪ DISQUALIFY | Remove from list |

### Scoring Formula
```
Base Score: 5 points
+ Website Problems (3 points each, max 9)
+ SaaS Opportunities (2 points each, max 8)
+ Growth Signals (2 points each, max 6)
+ Decision Maker Found (2 points)
+ Email Verified (2 points)
- Disqualifiers (-10 points each)
```

### Disqualifiers
- Enterprise website already (modern, optimized)
- <3 employees (too small)
- No identifiable contact
- Outside UK geography
- Inactive/closed business

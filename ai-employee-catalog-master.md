## ⚠️ COMPLIANCE & RISKS

### SMS Compliance (UK):
- ✅ Business hours only (9am-6pm)
- ✅ Opt-out option in every message
- ✅ B2B only (not consumer spam)
- ✅ Clear business identity

### GDPR:
- ✅ B2B contacts only
- ✅ Legitimate business interest
- ✅ Data processing agreement
- ✅ Customer can request deletion

### Platform ToS:
- ✅ Google My Business (API compliant)
- ✅ Twilio (registered business)
- ✅ Gmail (business account, not spam)

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- ❌ Job application bots (LinkedIn/Indeed ToS violations)
- ❌ Mass cold email spam (GDPR violations)
- ❌ Fake reviews (illegal)
- ❌ Scraping protected data (ToS violations)

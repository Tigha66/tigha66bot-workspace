## 📋 What It Does

### Workflow

```
[Lead Arrives] (form/email/WhatsApp)
       ↓
[AI responds in 60 seconds]
"Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out about [service]! 
Quick questions to help us serve you better..."
       ↓
[Asks 5 qualifying questions]
1. What service do you need? (repair/replacement/inspection)
2. What's your postcode? (check service area)
3. How urgent is this? (emergency/soon/later)
4. Residential or commercial?
5. Best phone number for callback?
       ↓
[AI scores the lead]
- In service area: +3 points
- Emergency/urgent: +3 points
- Correct service: +2 points
- Phone provided: +2 points
- Ready this week: +1 point

Score 0-4 = COLD (polite close)
Score 5-8 = WARM (offer callback)
Score 9+ = HOT (send booking link + alert owner)
       ↓
[Route the lead]
HOT → Send booking link + SMS owner
WARM → Ask final question + schedule follow-up
COLD → Polite close + log for future
       ↓
[Log everything to dashboard]
Owner sees: Lead details, score, status, next action
```

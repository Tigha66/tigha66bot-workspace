# 🚀 Quick Start: Launch Your Content Agency in 7 Days

## Day 1: Setup & Configuration

### Morning (2 hours)
- [ ] Set up OpenClaw agents (use configs in `/agents/`)
- [ ] Configure LLM access (Bailian/Qwen3.5-Plus)
- [ ] Set up Mission Control dashboard
- [ ] Create content templates

### Afternoon (2 hours)
- [ ] Create sample content portfolio
- [ ] Write 3 blog posts (showcase your quality)
- [ ] Create 10 social media posts
- [ ] Design 5 graphics (use Canva or AI)

### Evening (1 hour)
- [ ] Set up landing page (Carrd, Webflow, or simple HTML)
- [ ] Create Calendly account for booking calls
- [ ] Set up Stripe for payments

---

## Day 2: Define Your Offer

### Morning (2 hours)
- [ ] Choose your niche (SaaS, e-commerce, professional services)
- [ ] Define your 3 service tiers (see pricing below)
- [ ] Create service description document
- [ ] Set your pricing

### Afternoon (2 hours)
- [ ] Create case study (even if hypothetical initially)
- [ ] Write your unique value proposition
- [ ] Prepare sales deck (5 slides max)
- [ ] Create contract template

### Evening (1 hour)
- [ ] Practice your pitch (record yourself)
- [ ] Refine based on recording
- [ ] Prepare answers to common objections

---

## Day 3: Build Prospect List

### Morning (3 hours)
- [ ] Identify 100 ideal prospects
  - LinkedIn Sales Navigator
  - Crunchbase (recently funded)
  - Industry directories
  - Twitter/Instagram search

### Afternoon (2 hours)
- [ ] Research each prospect (10 min each)
- [ ] Note specific pain points
- [ ] Find personalization hooks
- [ ] Organize in spreadsheet/CRM

### Evening (1 hour)
- [ ] Draft personalized outreach messages
- [ ] Create 3 email templates
- [ ] Create 3 LinkedIn message templates
- [ ] Set up email tracking (Mixmax, HubSpot, etc.)

---

## Day 4: Launch Outreach

### Morning (2 hours)
- [ ] Send 50 LinkedIn connection requests
- [ ] Send 100 cold emails
- [ ] Post on LinkedIn about your service
- [ ] Share in relevant Facebook/Slack groups

### Afternoon (2 hours)
- [ ] Follow up on connections accepted
- [ ] Engage with prospect content (comment, like)
- [ ] Send 50 more connection requests
- [ ] Post Twitter thread about your service

### Evening (1 hour)
- [ ] Track responses
- [ ] Schedule any calls that came in
- [ ] Prepare for tomorrow's follow-ups
- [ ] Review and optimize messages

---

## Day 5: Sales Calls & Follow-ups

### Afternoon (2 hours)
- [ ] Take more sales calls

### Evening (1 hour)
- [ ] Review day's performance

- [ ] Prepare for tomorrow

## 💰 Pricing Calculator

```

- ~$0.002 per 1K tokens
- Average blog post: 2K tokens = $0.004
- Average social post: 200 tokens = $0.0004

Per Client Per Month:
- 30 blog posts: $0.12
- 100 social posts: $0.04
- 4 newsletters: $0.02
- Total LLM cost: ~$0.20

Your Time:
- Client management: 2 hours/month

At $5K/month pricing:
```

## 🚨 Common Objections & Responses

**"We already have an agency"**
→ "That's great! What are you currently spending? We typically save companies 70% while increasing output 3x. Worth a quick comparison?"

**"AI content isn't as good"**
→ "I thought the same thing until I saw the results. Our clients are getting 340% higher engagement than their previous human-written content. Happy to show you examples?"

**"We need to think about it"**
→ "Totally understand! What specific concerns do you have? I want to make sure I address them before you decide."

**"It's too expensive"**
→ "Compared to what you're currently spending on content (or the cost of hiring in-house), you're actually saving 70-80%. Plus, we guarantee results. What's your current content budget?"

**"Send me more information"**
→ "Absolutely! I'll send over our case studies and pricing. When would be a good time to follow up and answer any questions? [Get specific day/time]"

# Client Acquisition System

## Overview
Automated system to acquire content agency clients at scale

## 🎯 Target Client Profiles

### Ideal Client #1: SaaS Startup
**Characteristics:**
- 10-100 employees
- Recently funded (Seed/Series A)
- Need consistent content for growth
- Budget: $5K-15K/month
- Decision maker: CMO or Founder

**Where to Find:**
- LinkedIn (search: "CMO" + "SaaS" + "Series A")
- Crunchbase (recently funded companies)
- Product Hunt (launching products)
- Twitter (SaaS founders)

**Pain Points:**
- No time to create content
- Need to establish thought leadership
- Competitors out-contenting them
- Need leads from content

---

### Ideal Client #2: E-commerce Brand
**Characteristics:**
- $1M-10M revenue
- DTC (direct-to-consumer)
- Active on social media
- Budget: $3K-10K/month
- Decision maker: Founder or Marketing Director

**Where to Find:**
- Instagram (brands with 10K-100K followers)
- Facebook Ad Library (running ads = budget)
- Shopify stores (builtwith.com)
- TikTok (brands creating video content)

**Pain Points:**
- Need product descriptions
- Social media burnout
- Need user-generated content
- SEO for product pages

---

### Ideal Client #3: Professional Services
**Characteristics:**
- Law firms, consultants, agencies
- 5-50 employees
- High-ticket services ($5K+ per client)
- Budget: $5K-20K/month
- Decision maker: Managing Partner

**Where to Find:**
- LinkedIn (partners, principals)
- Industry associations
- Local business chambers
- Referrals from other professionals

**Pain Points:**
- Need to establish expertise
- Competitors ranking higher
- Need leads consistently
- No time to write

## 📧 Outreach Campaigns

### Campaign 1: LinkedIn Outreach
**Volume:** 100 connection requests/week

**Connection Request:**
```
Hi [Name], I noticed [Company] is doing great work in [industry]. 
I'm building an AI-powered content agency and would love to connect 
with innovative leaders like yourself. No pitch, just connecting!
```

**Follow-up Message 1 (Day 3):**
```
Thanks for connecting, [Name]! 

I've been following [Company]'s growth and noticed your content strategy. 
Quick question - are you currently working with a content agency or 
handling everything in-house?

Always curious how fast-growing companies approach content.
```

**Follow-up Message 2 (Day 7 - if they respond):**
```
That's interesting! 

We just helped [Similar Company] replace their $30K/month content 
agency with our AI-powered system. They're now getting 3x the content 
at half the cost.

Would you be open to a quick 15-min chat to see if we could do 
something similar for [Company]? No pressure at all.
```

**Follow-up Message 3 (Day 14 - if no response):**
```
[Name], one last message from me!

We're taking on 2 more clients this month for our content agency 
service. Given [Company]'s growth, thought it might be worth a chat.

If not, no worries at all - I'll stop reaching out. Either way, 
wishing you continued success! 🚀
```

## 💬 Sales Call Script

### Opening (2 minutes)
```
"Thanks for making time, [Name]!

Before we dive in, I want to make sure this is valuable for you.

What's your biggest challenge with content right now?
Is it time, quality, consistency, cost... what's the main pain point?"

[Listen carefully, take notes]
```

### Discovery (5 minutes)
```
"That makes total sense. A lot of companies we work with 
struggle with the same thing.

Can you tell me more about your current setup?
- Are you working with an agency or in-house team?
- How much are you currently spending?
- How satisfied are you with the results?
- What would ideal look like for you?"

[Let them talk, identify pain points]
```

### Presentation (5 minutes)
```
"Based on what you've shared, here's how we can help...

[Present solution tailored to their specific pain points]

We'd assign a team of AI agents to handle:
- [Specific service they need]
- [Specific service they need]
- [Specific service they need]

The result is [specific outcome they want]
without [specific pain point they mentioned].

Does that sound like it would solve the challenges you mentioned?"
```

### Pricing (3 minutes)
```
"Great! So here's how pricing works...

Our [Tier Name] package is $[X]/month and includes:
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]
- [Deliverable 4]

Compared to traditional agencies charging $15K-30K for 
the same work, you're saving [X]% while getting better results.

We also have a [higher/lower] tier at $[Y]/month if you need 
[more/less] content.

Which tier sounds like the better fit for where you're at?"
```

### Closing (5 minutes)
```
"Perfect! Here's what happens next...

1. We'll send over the agreement today
2. Once signed, we'll do an onboarding call
3. We'll analyze your brand voice and goals
4. First content goes out within 48 hours
5. You'll see results within the first week

Any questions about the process?

[Answer questions]

Great! I'll send the agreement to [email address].

Once you sign, we can get started as early as [date].

Sound good?

[Get commitment]

Awesome! Looking forward to working together, [Name]!

You're going to love the results. 🚀"
```

## 📊 Pipeline Management

### CRM Setup
```
Stage 1: Prospecting (500 contacts)
Stage 2: Outreach Sent (100/week)
Stage 3: Response Received (20% response rate)
Stage 4: Call Scheduled (50% of responses)
Stage 5: Proposal Sent (70% of calls)
Stage 6: Negotiation (50% of proposals)
Stage 7: Closed Won (70% of negotiations)
Stage 8: Onboarding (100% of closed won)
```

### Weekly Targets
```
New prospects added: 100
Outreach messages sent: 100
Responses received: 20
Calls scheduled: 10
Proposals sent: 7
Closed deals: 3-5
Revenue: $15K-25K/week
```

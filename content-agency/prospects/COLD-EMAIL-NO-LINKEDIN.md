## 🎯 HOW TO FIND EMAIL ADDRESSES (Without LinkedIn)

### Method 1: Company Website (5 min per prospect)

**Step 1:** Google the company
```
[Company Name] + "contact" or "team" or "about"
```

**Step 2:** Look for:
- Contact page
- Team/About page
- Footer email
- Press/Media contact

**Step 3:** Common email patterns:
```
first@company.com
first.last@company.com
f.last@company.com
firstname@company.com
contact@company.com
info@company.com (ask to be forwarded)
```

---

### Method 2: Google Search Operators (3 min per prospect)

**Search these exact queries:**

**Find team pages:**
```
"[Company Name]" + "team" OR "about" OR "leadership"
```

**Find email addresses:**
```
"[Company Name]" + "@[companydomain].com"
```

**Find contact info:**
```
"[Company Name]" + "contact" OR "email" OR "phone"
```

**Example:**
```
"TechStart SaaS" + "@techstart.com"
```

# ✅ Daily Prospecting Checklist

## Print This & Check Off Daily!

---

## 🌅 MORNING ROUTINE (30 min)

### Find Prospects (15 min)
- [ ] Search LinkedIn: "CMO" + "SaaS" + "Series A"
- [ ] Find 10 new companies
- [ ] Identify contact person (name + title)
- [ ] Get LinkedIn profile URL
- [ ] Find email (pattern: first@company.com)

### Research (15 min)
- [ ] Visit each company website
- [ ] Check their blog (last post date?)
- [ ] Check their LinkedIn (posting frequency?)
- [ ] Note 1 personalization hook per prospect
- [ ] Fill in prospect tracker

---

## ☀️ MIDDAY ROUTINE (30 min)

### Send Outreach (20 min)
- [ ] Send 10 LinkedIn connection requests
- [ ] Personalize each request (use template)
- [ ] Send 10 emails (if emails found)
- [ ] Track all in spreadsheet

### Engage (10 min)
- [ ] Comment on 5 prospect posts
- [ ] Like 10 prospect posts
- [ ] Share 1 relevant industry article
- [ ] Post on your own LinkedIn

---

## 🌆 AFTERNOON ROUTINE (30 min)

### Follow-Up (15 min)
- [ ] Follow up on Day 3 outreach
- [ ] Follow up on Day 7 outreach
- [ ] Respond to any replies immediately
- [ ] Schedule calls for interested prospects

### Sales Calls (15 min)
- [ ] Take scheduled calls (aim for 2-3/day)
- [ ] Send proposal within 1 hour after call
- [ ] Track call outcomes in CRM
- [ ] Schedule follow-up if needed

---

## 🌙 EVENING ROUTINE (15 min)

### Review & Plan (15 min)
- [ ] Count today's numbers:
  - Outreach sent: ___ / 20
  - Responses: ___
  - Calls scheduled: ___
  - Proposals sent: ___
- [ ] Update prospect tracker
- [ ] Plan tomorrow's 10 prospects
- [ ] Celebrate 1 win from today!

## 📞 WHEN YOU GET STUCK

**No responses?**
→ Improve your personalization
→ Change your subject lines
→ Try different prospects
→ Ask for feedback

**Getting calls but no closes?**
→ Improve your sales pitch
→ Lower your prices temporarily
→ Add more social proof
→ Offer guarantee

**No time?**
→ Wake up 1 hour earlier
→ Do 10 min instead of 30
→ Outsource research ($5/hr VA)
→ Automate what you can

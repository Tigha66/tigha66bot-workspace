# 📧 Personalized Outreach Templates

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Choose the right template** based on prospect type
2. **Personalize the bracketed sections** [like this]
3. **Keep it short** - busy people skim
4. **Include ONE clear CTA** (call-to-action)
5. **Follow up 3x** before giving up

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Best Times to Send
- **Tuesday-Thursday:** 9-11 AM or 2-4 PM
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

### Personalization That Works
- Mention their recent post
- Congratulate on funding
- Note specific observation about their company
- Reference mutual connection (if any)

### What Gets Responses
- ✅ Short messages (under 100 words)
- ✅ Specific compliments
- ✅ Clear value proposition
- ✅ Low-pressure CTA

### What Gets Ignored
- ❌ Generic "I'd like to add you to my network"
- ❌ Long paragraphs
- ❌ Hard sell in first message
- ❌ No personalization

## ⏰ MINUTE 10-25: Find 10 Prospects

### LinkedIn Search #1 (5 min)
**Search:** `"CMO" AND "SaaS" AND "Series A"`

**Filter by:**
- People
- Past Month (for recent activity)
- United States (or your timezone)

**Find 5 people who:**
- Posted about funding/growth recently
- Work at 20-200 employee company
- Look active on LinkedIn

### LinkedIn Search #2 (5 min)
**Search:** `"VP Marketing" AND "B2B" AND "Software"`

**Find 3 more people**

### LinkedIn Search #3 (5 min)

**Find 2 more people**

**You now have 10 prospects!**

## ⏰ MINUTE 25-40: Research Each One

### For Each Prospect (90 seconds each)
1. **Open their LinkedIn profile**
2. **Note:** Company name, size, recent posts
3. **Visit company website** - check blog
4. **Find 1 personalization hook:**
   - Recent funding announcement
   - Post they made
   - Company milestone
   - Industry trend they mentioned
5. **Guess their email:**
   - first@company.com
   - first.last@company.com
   - f.last@company.com

**Fill in your tracker as you go!**

## ⏰ MINUTE 40-55: Send Outreach

### LinkedIn Connection Requests (10 min)
**Use this template:**

```

I help SaaS companies scale content 3x without hiring. 
Just helped [Similar Company] go from 0 to 50K visitors in 90 days.

Would love to connect and share what's working.

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Send to all 10 prospects**

### Emails (5 min - if you found emails)
**Use this template:**

```
Subject: Quick question about [Company]'s content

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Send to all prospects where you found email**

## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Best Times to Send
- **LinkedIn:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM
- **Email:** Tuesday-Thursday, 2-4 PM
- **Follow-up:** 3 days after initial

### Best Personalization
- Mention their recent post
- Congratulate on funding
- Note a specific observation
- Reference mutual connection

### What NOT to Do
- Don't send generic "I'd like to add you to my network"
- Don't pitch in first message
- Don't write a novel (keep it short)
- Don't forget to follow up!

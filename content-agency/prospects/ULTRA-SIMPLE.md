```

2. Click "Compose" (top left)
```

## ⚡ STEP 2: Find 5 Companies (5 minutes)

### Google Search #1:
```
"Series A SaaS companies 2026"
```

### For Each Company:
1. Click their website
2. Click "Team" or "About" or "Contact"
3. Find a name (CMO, VP Marketing, Founder)
4. Write down: **Name + Company**

**Do this 5 times = 5 prospects!**

## ⚡ STEP 3: Get Their Emails (3 minutes)

3. Build email: john@company.com

- john.smith@company.com

get 5x more reviews even though they were already asking."

**"Too expensive"**
→ "Actually, it pays for itself with just ONE extra customer from the 
higher Google ranking. Most users get 10-20 extra reviews/month, which 
typically means 5-10 extra calls, which means 2-3 extra customers.

**"We don't do tech"**
→ "Totally get it! That's why I made it stupid simple. You give me your 
customer list and Google review link, I set everything up. You literally

→ "I hear that a lot. What happened? [Listen] Yeah, that's awful. Here's 
my promise: 14-day free trial, no card. Try it, see the reviews come in, 
then decide. If it doesn't work, you've lost nothing. Fair?"

→ "Absolutely! What's the best email? [Get email] Sending now — subject 
line is 'ReviewFlow Pro Demo'. Check it out and let me know if you have 
questions. What's the best number to reach you if I need to follow up?"

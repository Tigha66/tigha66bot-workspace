## 📊 WHATSAPP VS EMAIL COMPARISON

| Metric | Email | WhatsApp |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Open rate | 20-30% | 90-98% |
| Response rate | 5-10% | 30-50% |
| Speed | Hours | Minutes |
| Formality | High | Low |
| Best for | Initial outreach | Follow-up |

**Strategy:** Email first, WhatsApp follow-up if no response after 3 days.

❌ Don't send generic copy-paste messages (personalize!)
❌ Don't send more than 3 follow-ups
❌ Don't send voice notes (unless they ask)

❌ Don't be pushy or aggressive

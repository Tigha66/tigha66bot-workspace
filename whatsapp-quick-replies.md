## 💡 WHATSAPP BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
- ✅ Reply within 1 hour (shows you're responsive)
- ✅ Use emojis (makes it friendly)
- ✅ Keep messages short (easy to read on mobile)
- ✅ Include Stripe link in every serious conversation
- ✅ Use their name (personal touch)

### DON'T:
- ❌ Send walls of text (break into chunks)
- ❌ Wait 24 hours to reply (strike while hot)
- ❌ Forget to include payment link (make it easy)
- ❌ Sound robotic (be human, be friendly)
- ❌ Push too hard (let them decide)
